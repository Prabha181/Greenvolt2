    Locate Our Showroom

    Our showroom is conveniently located at Dediyasan GIDC, easily accessible from all parts of Mahesana and surrounding areas.

    Directions to Our Showroom

    1

    From Mahesana Railway Station: Take the Modhera Road exit and drive for approximately 5 km towards Dediyasan GIDC.

    2

    From Ahmedabad: Take NH27 towards Mahesana, then follow signs to Dediyasan GIDC area.

    Landmark: Look for Swagat Residency - our showroom is directly opposite to it.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick Answers to Common Questions

    Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about visiting our showroom and our services.

    What are your showroom timings?

    We're open Monday-Saturday 9 AM to 8 PM, and Sundays 10 AM to 6 PM. We're also available for appointments outside these hours.

    Do you offer test drives?

    Yes! We encourage test drives. You can book online or call us. Just bring a valid driving license and we'll arrange everything.

    What financing options are available?

    We offer various financing solutions including bank loans, easy EMI options, and government subsidy assistance. Our team will help you find the best option.

    Do you provide home delivery?

    Yes, we offer doorstep delivery within Mahesana and surrounding areas. Additional charges may apply for distant locations.

    Emergency Support

    Need Immediate Assistance?

    Our emergency support team is available 24/7 for existing customers who need immediate technical assistance or roadside support.
